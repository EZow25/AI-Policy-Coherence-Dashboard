AI Policy Coherence: Southeast Asia

What is AI Policy Coherence?

AI Policy Coherence measures how closely national AI policies align with shared regional AI governance principles.

What are the AI Governance Principles? ▾

Southeast Asia's ASEAN Guide on AI Governance and Ethics breaks down AI governance into 7 principles, each with their own sub-principles.

How is AI Policy Coherence Quantified? ▾

We follow a structured scoring framework based on the Southeast Asia's ASEAN Guide on AI Governance and Ethics.
To generate an overall coherence score for each country, we use a bottom-up approach starting with sub-principles and the country's AI policies. We first aim to assign a score from 0 to 3 for each sub-principle based on how strongly the country's policies adhere to those sub-principles. Afterward, sub-principles belonging to the same principle are used to generate a coherence score for that principle. Those principle scores are finally averaged to generate the country's overall coherence score.
